# Contributing

- **Branch off `main`**: name it `MCM-XX-short-slug` (e.g. `MCM-34-image-cnn`).
- **Reference the issue** in the branch / commit / PR title (`MCM-XX`) so it links on the YouTrack board.
- **Open a PR into `main`** — CI (`test`) must be green to merge. **No direct pushes to `main`** (admins may bypass for hotfixes).
- **Never commit** secrets, SA keys, `.env`, or large data — they're gitignored.
- Keep tasks small; update the relevant KB article when you change a contract or behaviour.
